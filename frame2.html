ICET

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET), located in Muvattupuzha, Kerala, is a private, self-financing institution founded in 2002 by the
Ilahia Trust. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University (KTU). The college is situated on a 25-acre campus in a tranquil, green setting.
