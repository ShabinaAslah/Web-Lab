ILAHIA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET), located in Muvattupuzha, Kerala, is a private, self-financing institution founded in 2002 by the Ilahia Trust. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University (KTU). The college is situated on a 25-acre campus in a tranquil, green setting.

Academic programs

ICET offers a range of undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA) programs.
B.Tech programs:
  1. Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
  2. Biomedical Engineering
  3. Civil Engineering
  4. Computer Science and Engineering
  5. Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security)
  6. Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  7. Electronics and Communication Engineering
  8. Food Technology
  9. Mechanical Engineering
  10. Robotics and Automation
Postgraduate programs:
  • M.Tech: Specializations include Computer Aided and Structural Engineering and Computer Science and Engineering.
  • MBA: Offered through the Ilahia School of Management Studies (ISMS).
