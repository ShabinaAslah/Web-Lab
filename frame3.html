DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER APPLICATION

The Department of Computer Applications at Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology offers a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. Established in 2008, the department aims to provide quality education and professional training in computer applications, preparing students for careers in the IT industry. It emphasizes a strong academic curriculum, industry interaction, research, and skill development through workshops, seminars, and placement support, helping students excel in both technical knowledge and practical experience.
